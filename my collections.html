Bene(600i)

Overview
Manufacturer: Benelli
Model: 600i
Type: Sportbike / Naked
Engine and Performance
Engine: 600cc inline-four engine
Power Output: Approximately 80 horsepower
Torque: Around 54 Nm
Cooling System: Liquid-cooled
Fuel System: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI)
Design and Features
Chassis: Lightweight trellis frame, enhancing agility and stability
Suspension:
Front: Upside-down forks (USD) for improved handling
Rear: Mono-shock adjustable suspension
Brakes:
Front: Dual disc brakes with 4-piston calipers
Rear: Single disc brake
Wheels: Lightweight alloy wheels
Tires: Sport-oriented tires for better grip and performance
Dimensions and Weight
Seat Height: Approximately 800 mm, making it accessible for various riders
Weight: Around 200 kg (wet), contributing to its nimble handling
Technology and Features
Instrumentation: Digital display showing speed, RPM, fuel gauge, and more
Lighting: LED headlight and taillight for improved visibility and aesthetics
ABS: Anti-lock braking system for enhanced safety
Riding Experience
The Benelli 600i is designed for riders who appreciate a balance of performance and comfort. It’s suitable for both city commuting and spirited rides on twisty roads. The bike’s sporty styling and responsive handling make it an attractive option for enthusiasts looking for an affordable yet capable motorcycle.

Jawa(classic-350)

Overview
Manufacturer: Jawa Motorcycles
Model: Classic 350
Type: Cruiser / Retro
Engine and Performance
Engine: 348cc single-cylinder, air-cooled engine
Power Output: Approximately 30 horsepower at 8,000 rpm
Torque: About 32 Nm at 4,500 rpm
Fuel System: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI)
Transmission: 6-speed gearbox
Design and Features
Chassis: Steel tubular frame, designed for stability and comfort
Suspension:
Front: Telescopic forks with a travel of 130 mm
Rear: Twin gas shock absorbers with a travel of 80 mm
Brakes:
Front: 280 mm disc brake
Rear: 240 mm disc brake
Wheels: Classic spoked wheels
Tires: 90/90-18 front and 120/80-17 rear for a comfortable ride
Dimensions and Weight
Seat Height: Approximately 765 mm, making it accessible for a wide range of riders
Weight: Around 172 kg (wet), contributing to its manageable handling
Technology and Features
Instrumentation: Semi-digital speedometer with an analog tachometer and trip meter
Lighting: Classic round headlamp with LED lighting for improved visibility
Colors: Available in various retro-inspired color options
Riding Experience
The Jawa Classic 350 is designed for riders who appreciate a mix of nostalgia and modern performance. Its comfortable seating position and classic styling make it ideal for leisurely rides through the city or along country roads. The bike's thumping single-cylinder engine provides a distinctive sound and ample torque for relaxed cruising.
